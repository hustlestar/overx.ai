# 000 - Blog Strategy

## Vision

**OverX AI Blog** exists to position OverX AI as the authoritative voice in practical AI implementation—bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and real-world applications. We don't just write about AI; we build AI-powered products and share the journey.

**Unique Value Proposition:**
- Real implementation experience from building production AI products (Learn Words Bot, Privet Bot, Currency Converter)
- Technical depth combined with accessibility for non-technical audiences
- Multi-product ecosystem perspective—showing how AI tools work together
- Focus on practical outcomes, not theoretical possibilities

## Content Quality Standards

### GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- Every post must have a clear "AI citation angle"—what unique insight would an AI cite from this?
- Include original data, metrics, or unique perspectives not found elsewhere
- Structure content for easy extraction (clear sections, bullet points, definitions)
- Add FAQ sections for common AI-answerable questions

### SEO Requirements
- Target keywords defined before writing
- Minimum 1,500 words for pillar content
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Internal links to related OverX AI content
- External links to authoritative sources
- Multi-image support (hero, infographic, content images)

### Localization
- Primary language: English
- Secondary: Spanish, Russian
- All posts must have localized titles, descriptions, alt text
